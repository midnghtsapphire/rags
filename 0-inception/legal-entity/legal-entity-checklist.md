## Step 1: Choose Business Structure

### Decision Criteria

| Structure | Best For | Pros | Cons | Tax | Cost |
|-----------|----------|------|------|-----|------|
| **Sole Proprietorship** | Solo freelancers | Simple, cheap | No liability protection | Personal income | $0-50 |
| **LLC** | Most startups | Liability protection, flexible | More paperwork | Pass-through or Corp | $50-500 |
| **S-Corp** | Profitable businesses | Tax savings on profits | Strict requirements | Pass-through | $100-800 |
| **C-Corp** | VC-funded startups | Investor-friendly, stock options | Double taxation, complex | Corporate | $100-800 |

### My Choice:
- [ ] Sole Proprietorship
- [ ] LLC
- [ ] S-Corp
- [ ] C-Corp
