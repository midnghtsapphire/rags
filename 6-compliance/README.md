# Phase 6: Compliance - Regulatory & Security

**Purpose:** Ensure legal and regulatory compliance  
**Duration:** Ongoing  
**Cost:** $5,000-50,000/year  
**When:** Before launch and continuously

## Compliance Types
- SOX (if public company)
- HIPAA (if healthcare data)
- GDPR (if EU users)
- SOC 2 (for SaaS)
- AI Ethics (for AI products)

## Subfolders
- `/sox/` - Financial controls
- `/hipaa/` - Healthcare compliance
- `/gdpr/` - EU privacy
- `/ai-ethics/` - Responsible AI
- `/patents/` - IP management
- `/security/` - Security policies
